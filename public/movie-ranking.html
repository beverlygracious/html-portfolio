Best Movies According to Beverly

My top 3 movies of all time

Howl's Moving Castle

For someone who enjoys art and music, this movie is a delight. The art style is just chefs kisses and the music just blends in with every scene to make it an adventure for the viewer.

Miracle in Cell No.7

This one is a total tear jerker. I started watching this thinking it is a comedy but ended up balling crying at the beautiful story between a mental disabled father and his daughter. If you wanna cry, go watch this one. Totally recommend.

Demon Slayer: Mugen Train

When the movie released, there were people posting videos online saying you will cry watching this movie and I being me, refused to believe this. Long and behold, when I finally watched the movie after completing the first season, I understood what they really meant.
Once again the art style and the animation that went into this one is just chefs kisses.
